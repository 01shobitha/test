7. Conclusion

VideoLAN can do much more that what is explained in this Quickstart. For example, vls can stream from a Satellite DVB card or an MPEG encoding card. VideoLAN can also stream multiple channels on a network that doesn't support multicast. And vlc can be used to do an image-wall !

To know about the full possibilities of VideoLAN, please read the other documentation.
