How to create a resizable window

This tutorial will show you how to create a resizable window with VLC Skin Editor.
It will show only how a background can made resizable. Resizing from the right side, bottom side and bottom left corner is covered.

Please note that this tutorial does not completely cover all aspects of resizable windows. A complete tutorial will be put up in the future.

1. Preparation

First take a png-file you want for your background. Then make a window with a window layout and place your background in that window.


2. Define maximum width and height

In the example the background image is 400x300 pixels. Because the layout must be resizable open the
layout settings by double-clicking on Resizable-layout:



With the Dimensions you can set the maximum/minimum width and height of the layout. Choose it as you like.

3. Glue corners

In the Items Window, now open the Image Settings for the image Resizable Background.




Set the general attributes Lefttop and Rightbottom. Lefttop of the background sticks to the left top and
rightbottom to the rightbottom.
When you set Action to move, you can move your window around your screen while holding right mouse button.

4. Adding the grips

The next step is to add the grips where you can get the window to resize it. In this example these grips
will be a square in the bottom left corner and two bars. One on the bottom side and one on the right side.
To make the grips add the SubBitmaps Bottomright, Bottombar and Rightbar to the bitmap background in
the Resources window.


Adding a SubBitmap requires to give the top left hand coordinates and the dimensions of the SubBitMap.
The bottom right square is 20x20 pixels, the Rightbar 20x260, depending on the height of the background.




When you finish the addition of the subbitmaps your resources window should look like:




When the subbitmaps are defined add them to the resizable layout in the Itemslist as Images.
Each time a subbitmap is added fill in their attributes.



The bottom right hand resizer should stay in the bottom right corner stick both Lefttop and RightBottom to rightbottom.
Now choose Action: resizeSE. This is horizontal and vertical resizing.
The bottombarresizer should stick with the corners to leftbotttom and rightbottom. Choose resizeS for vertical resizing.
The rightbarresizer should stick with the top leftcorner and botom rightcorner. Choose resizeE for horizontal resizing.

The final result will be something like this:


5. Finish

Save your work and export the skin as vlt file. If you open VLC, you can go to the newly made skin and see that it works!
