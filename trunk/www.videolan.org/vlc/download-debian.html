    • VLC for Debian GNU/Linux

    VLC is now part of the Debian unstable distribution (sid), simply use your usual Debian mirror to download the latest packages of VLC!

    However, libdvdcss is not in the Debian distributions because of legal concerns but you might need that package to watch your encrypted DVD.

    Add the following lines to your /etc/apt/sources.list:

       deb     ftp://ftp.videolan.org/pub/videolan/debian $(ARCH)/
   deb-src ftp://ftp.videolan.org/pub/videolan/debian sources/

    For a normal install, do:

       # apt-get update
   # apt-get install gnome-vlc libdvdcss2
