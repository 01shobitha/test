VLC media player MacOS X nightly builds

Due to sorting conventions, the newest builds are at the bottom of the index. Remember to test the latest nightly build before reporting a bug (in one of the older nighly builds). Have fun !

Please report nightly builds breakage (meaning that there is no new build) on #videolan on irc.freenode.net .

Because of certain limitations of one of our build machines, the current builds are 10.4-only. Compatibility with 10.3.9 will be restored until the next stable release.
