List of CSS-free DVDs

Introduction

Some (most of, actually) DVDs are encrypted. While this is not a problem for VLC on most architectures, a few operating systems still don't provide the necessary interface to decrypt DVDs.

For people worried about decrypting DVDs, we used to maintain a list of unencrypted DVDs. Please note that this list has been discontinued because of the amount of work it needed.

See also the OpenDVD website for much more stuff related to this subject. Tom Vogt's DeCSS central has also lots of interesting resources.
