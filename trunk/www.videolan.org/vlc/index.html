VLC - the cross-platform media player

Overview

VLC (VideoLAN Client) is a highly portable multimedia player for various audio and video formats (MPEG, MPEG-2, MPEG-4, DivX, mp3, ogg, ...) as well as DVDs, VCDs, and various streaming protocols.

For more information about what features are supported on your operating system, please see the full features list. You can also have a look at some VLC screenshots.
