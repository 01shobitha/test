Contacting the VideoLAN team

User questions can be sent to the appropriate mailing-lists:

  • vlc@videolan.org for specific questions about vlc.
  • vls@videolan.org for specific questions about vls.
  • vlcs@videolan.org for specific questions about vlcs and the network.

Developper's questions can be sent to the appropriate mailing-lists:

  • vlc-devel@videolan.org for specific questions about vlc.
  • vls-devel@videolan.org for specific questions about vls.
  • vlcs-devel@videolan.org for specific questions about network tools.

If you want to contact the French team, about partnerships for example, write to videolan@videolan.org.
