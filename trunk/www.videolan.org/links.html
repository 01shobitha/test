Projets used in VideoLAN

  • ffmpeg : a free MPEG-4/DivX/OpenDivX codec.
  • Xvid : a free MPEG-4 video codec.
  • libmpeg2 : a free MPEG-2 video stream decoder.
  • liba52 : a free ATSC A/52 stream decoder.
  • libdvdrea d : a free DVD reading library.
  • Ogg Vorbis : a free and patent-free audio format and codec.
  • FLAC : a free and patent-free lossless audio codec.
  • Theora : a free and patent-free video format and codec.
  • FAAC : free MPEG-4 audio codecs.
  • Mad : a free MPEG layer 1, 2 and 3 audio decoder.
  • libdv : a free codec for DV video, the encoding format used by most digital camcorders.
  • OpenSLP : a free implementation of the Service Location Protocol (SLP).
  • linuxtv.org : free Linux drivers for Satellite, Digitial Terrestial Television and MPEG encoding cards.
  • libdvb : a free library to access the DVB drivers.
  • Video4Linux : the video capture API of the Linux kernel.
  • IvyTV : free Linux drivers for for the iTVC15 family of MPEG codecs, found in the Hauppauge WinTV-PVR-250.
